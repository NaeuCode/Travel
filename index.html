The traditional dress for Turkmen men is the "kaftan" or "chapan," a long robe-like garment usually made of silk or wool, with intricate embroidery. Women wear colorful dresses called "yukata" and wrap their heads with veils. Music and dance are integral parts of Turkmen culture. They have a variety of traditional musical instruments like dutar (a type of lute), tambur, and gidjak. Dance forms like the "Lezgi" are popular.
